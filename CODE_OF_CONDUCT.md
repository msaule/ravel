# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Ravel aims to be a welcoming, respectful, and constructive open-source project.
Contributors, maintainers, and community members should be able to participate
without harassment or hostility.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and generous in feedback.
- Assume good intent while still being honest about technical risks.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, documentation, and behavior, not on people.
- Help keep provider/auth claims accurate and avoid overstating unsupported features.
- Prefer safe, reproducible workflows when discussing analytics or code execution.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary conduct.
- Publishing other people's private information without permission.
- Deliberately misleading users about provider support, pricing, or auth boundaries.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code of conduct, email
Markuss Saule at <markusstomas@gmail.com>.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict
participation when needed to protect the community and the project.
